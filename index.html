Jhosua Oajaca

I'm currently a senior at Shortridge High School. I'm originally from Guatemala and I came to Indiana when I was 11 years old. Ever since I came to the U.S, I had a curiosity for technology and the developement of software. This year, I had a great opportunity with the Nextech Catapult program to explore and develop technical skills and explore carreers in the tech field here in the center of Indianapolis.

One of the hardest situations that really affected me was moving from my foreign country Guatemala to Indianapolis with my family 5 years ago due to our financial issues and limited education offered in Guatemala. I started elementary school without any English experience, but I overcame my issues as I worked hard every day to learn fluent English. In a period of only 3 years, I was able to fluently speak, write, read, and listen to English. I was able to complete my goal with the help of my family, who was always there for me when I need them the most.
